Food

In this section we will explore the various locations around Dublin where you can find delicious, authentic Brazilian food.


Restaurants

Wigwam

From bottomless brunch to a la carte, the Brazilian inspired mouth watering dishes are not to be missed. The cocktails are also divine.

BAH33

If you want to experience the authentic Gaucho BBQ, otherwise known as "churrasco", BAH33 is the place to be! Get on it quick!

Wasabi Bar & Grill

Have you ever tried sushi with a Brazilian twist? Now's your chance! Wasabi has a wide selection of dishes to cater for all needs.

Snacks

O Brasileiro Shop

If you've never tried a "coxinha" before, O Brasileiro Shop have the tastiest ones in town! As well as an extensive range of Brazilian treats.

Churros do Lulu

Who doesn't love churros? Especially when they are covered in all of the craziest toppings you can imagine! An absolute dream.

Pastel King

Hidden away in the Oriental Pantry on Moore Street, this gem of a place serves the best pastels you will find in Dublin!
